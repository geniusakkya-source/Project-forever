Struggle Vs Compromise

You chose stability over struggle,
and I understand.

I wasn’t rich enough for your world,
but I was sincere enough to build one with you.

You saw my present,
not my potential.

Today, I walk away with less money,
but more determination.

Someday, when my reality matches my dreams,
I won’t ask why you left —
but you might wonder why you did.

(26-03-2026)
